Recruitment process

A serious process for serious candidates.

We assess candidates based on professional attitude, written communication, ethics, attention to detail, technical potential, digital confidence and ability to learn. A polished CV matters, but evidence of discipline matters more.

1. Submit your profile

Send your CV, short motivation, area of interest, qualifications, experience, portfolio links where relevant and your availability.

2. Screening and shortlisting

Applications may be reviewed for clarity, role fit, communication quality, technical foundation, ethical awareness and ability to work in a professional environment.

3. Assessment or discussion

Suitable candidates may be invited for a written task, technical discussion, portfolio review, interview or practical assessment depending on the pathway.

Application guidance

How to make your application stronger.

A strong application should show evidence of competence, seriousness, writing ability and readiness to work in a professional services environment.
