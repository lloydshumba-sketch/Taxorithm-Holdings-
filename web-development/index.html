Before we start

Information needed for a proper web project.

A good website depends on clear information. Before development begins, clients should prepare the business name, services, contact details, preferred colours, logo, images, target clients and any pages required.

Business and service details

Provide your business name, service list, target market, location, contact details, email addresses and any legal or policy details you want shown.

Brand and design direction

Provide your logo, preferred colours, examples of websites you like, images, tone of language and whether the site must look corporate, modern, premium or simple.

Technical setup

Provide domain access, hosting access, email routing requirements, form destination emails and any platform preference such as static hosting, GitHub Pages or other deployment options.
