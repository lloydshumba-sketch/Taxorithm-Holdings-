Governance

Privacy, ethics and payment verification.

Taxorithm applies governance principles designed to protect client information, promote ethical conduct and reduce payment fraud risk.

Privacy Policy

Taxorithm collects client information only where necessary to respond to enquiries, assess engagements, deliver services, maintain records and comply with applicable legal or regulatory obligations.

  • Information collected may include names, contact details, company information, tax records, accounting documents, transaction data and service correspondence.
  • Information is used for consultation, service delivery, client administration, billing, compliance and communication.
  • Client information is not sold to third parties.
  • Information may be shared only where required for service delivery, legal compliance, professional advice, payment administration or authorised client instructions.
  • Website enquiries and email correspondence are handled as confidential business communications.
  • Clients may request correction of inaccurate information or clarification of information held for engagement purposes.

Code of Ethics

Taxorithm’s professional conduct is guided by integrity, objectivity, professional competence, due care, confidentiality and professional behaviour.

  • Integrity: communications and deliverables must be honest, clear and not misleading.
  • Objectivity: professional judgement must not be compromised by bias, conflict of interest or undue influence.
  • Professional competence and due care: work must be performed with appropriate technical care and diligence.
  • Confidentiality: client information must be protected and used only for legitimate engagement purposes.
  • Professional behaviour: Taxorithm seeks to comply with applicable laws, regulations and responsible business practices.
  • Anti-bribery and anti-corruption: improper inducements, unethical facilitation or dishonest client instructions are not accepted.
  • Responsible technology and data use: client data should be handled with care when used in digital systems, analytics or reporting tools.

Payment Requests

Taxorithm encourages all clients to verify payment requests before making payment. Official payments should be linked to a valid invoice, quotation, engagement confirmation or written service instruction.

  • Do not pay based only on an informal message from an unknown or unverified number.
  • Confirm invoice references and official Taxorithm communication details before payment.
  • Report suspicious payment requests to info@taxorithm.us immediately.
  • Refunds, billing corrections and overpayments are handled through a formal review process.
  • Taxorithm does not knowingly authorise misleading, fraudulent or unauthorised payment instructions.
